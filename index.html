About

I write globally accessible support articles, engaging marketing blog posts, and an up-to-date development changelog. I have worn many "hats" over the years: user journey mapper, process designer, and site copy author. Working in a fully remote environment with a flexible schedule, I am a self-starter with a proven record of content excellence.
