About

I create accessible, engaging, and effective content for users around the world. My goal is to connect people to the information that they want in a frustration-free way. We live in an age of information where accessibility is too often under-valued. I see this as a challenge-- one that I happily rise to.

I am a Senior Product Specialist for a nonprofit SaaS (Amara Subtitling) where I create and manage documentation for users of a SaaS product. I am the go-to proofreader for my team and I created my own set of Guidelines for Global Communication.

I have a proven track record of writing globally accessible support articles, engaging marketing blog posts, and an up-to-date development changelog. I have worn many "hats" over the years: user journey mapper, process designer, and site copy author. Working in a fully remote environment with a flexible schedule, I am a self-starter who holds myself to a high standard of content excellence.
